Welcome to Autosoft

Autosoft India Engineering Pvt. Ltd., (ASI) is an associate company of Sunwell Solutions Co. Ltd., (Sunwells) which is head quartered in Japan. ASI acts as an Offshore Delivery Centre (ODC) for the staffing needs and Offshore Development Centre (ODC) for engineering services needs of Sunwells.

Sunwells Solutions Co. Ltd., a pioneer in providing Project Management Services and Engineering Design Services has bagged a product development project worth 30,000 hours from Isuzu Motors, Japan. A NRI part of the Sunwells board has felt a need to form an Offshore Development Centre (ODC) in India to reap the benefit of lower cost of operations and access to the qualified talent pool. Autosoft India Engineering Pvt. Ltd., has emerged as a trusted partner to cater the needs of Sunwells and emerged as a successful ODC. Isuzu Motors, after the successful completion of the said project has admired and appreciated the efforts of ASI. (Link of appreciation letter)

Later when Sunwells ventured into staffing, to supply qualified engineers specialized in Plant Engineering and CAD/CAE engineers providing product design and development to the leading global engineering conglomerates that were headquartered in Japan, ASI has become a natural partner to support Sunwells.
